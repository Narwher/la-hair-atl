About Us

The Hair Genie has developed a method of enhancing your hair to make it look longer and or fuller without damaging glues and adhesives. Fusion Extensions, Peel & Stick (Tape in) Extensions, Strand by Strand Extensions, Metal beads, and Clip-in Extensions... all of these methods will damage your own hair.

Whether you need a little help with your hair or a lot of help with your hair, this is the place.

My “Micro Trac” hand sewn method is glue less and does not cause damage. This unique method was developed specifically to meet the needs of smart, glamorous, busy, career, professional and business ladies of all nationalities.

We always match your hair to the texture and color of your own hair. We even match “Mixed Chicks”. We are not a celebrity salon. We don't have celebrity prices. We offer high end, quality, affordable, proven user friendly human hair extensions.

We never damage your precious hair. In fact, if your hair is damaged, we'll help you recover it. Style it in a minute. We’ll show you how. Ask us about the “Genie Roll ”.
