The "Micro-Trak" System Allows easy access to your scalp for proper hygiene. A healthy scalp promotes healthy hair growth and recovery, as well as allows you to grow, groom and maintain your own natural hair. This low maintenance method will not damage your hair. The Advanced Tracking System allows Quick and Easy Tightening. You can even do a chemical touch-up (relaxer, perm, or color) without removing these tiny "Micro-traks".
